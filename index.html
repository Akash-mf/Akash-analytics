About Me

I'm Akash, a data analytics professional with a deep passion for teaching and empowering beginners. I specialize in SQL, Python, Excel, Power BI, and Data Visualization. Data skills are the key to unlocking better career opportunities and smarter decision-making.

My journey started just like many of yours – with curiosity, limited resources, and a strong desire to grow. I completed my Master's in Computer Application from Anna University and gained valuable hands-on experience during my time at Binare IT Solution, Chennai.

I know what it's like to feel lost in the world of data. That’s why I’ve built Akash Analytics – an online learning platform designed to take you from zero to confident data analyst with real-world projects and personal mentorship.
